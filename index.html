WELCOME TO BAUST

Bangladesh Army University of Science & Technology (BAUST), the pioneer technical institutes of Armed Forces, started its journey from 15th February 2015. It was the visionary leadership of the honorable prime minister of People’s Republic of Bangladesh Sheikh Hasina to establish a technical institute of Armed Forces. BAUST is located at Saidpur Cantonment. Saidpur is a city of Nilphamari district in Rangpur Division of Bangladesh. The city becomes a very important communication hub for adjoining major district headquarters. BAUST (Saidpur) has well equipped class rooms with multimedia and web camera with internet facilities and Laboratories with modern equipment. Message From VC

Bangladesh Army University of Science and Technology (BAUST), Saidpur founded by the Bangladesh Army with due permission from the Government and UGC. As this university is fully patronized by the Bangladesh Army, it will soon establish itself as a prestigious university in the field of Engineering, Technology, Business and Humanities at home and abroad. The mission of BAUST is to develop human resources in the different fields of education to meet the ever changing needs of the society. This University aims at producing quality graduates at affordable costs imbued with the spirit of ethical values and equipped with intellectual knowledge. Surely our graduates will be highly suited to face the top-ranked modern world challenges.

Programming Club

BAUST Programming Club is a community of students highly enthusiastic about coding, preparation for ACM ICPC, NCPC and other national and international level programming contest& software competition, ethical hacking, Web Designing and all other aspects of programming. We organize activities related to Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development), programming contests etc.. Seminars and lecture are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, development, among other things. Apart from this, we also develop various applications to be used by the campus. Every semester we offer a large number of projects to the first year students through our software development project I course. The summer2016 saw close to 15 applicants and Fall 2015 saw 25 applications as well.

BAUST Debating Society

“যুক্তির ধারে শাণিত হোক মেধা” with this slogan BAUST Debating Society has started its journey from April 2015. From its establishment BAUST Debating Society has initiated and successfully operated lots of programs like Fun debate, Parliamentary debate, and old style debate, workshop and discussion session on debate. In Spring 2016 semester BAUST Debating Society has arranges an Intra-University Debate Competition and a Judge Training Session. More over the student from BAUST Debating Society has participated in different national level debate competition and achieve very prestigious position.
